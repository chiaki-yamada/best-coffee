ABOUT

Since 1989 Best Gourmet Coffee Company has been serving our customers with premium quality coffee at an affordable price all the while focusing on sustainability and positive outcomes. Our family owned company has been roasting coffee for generations of Canadians providing good jobs for people here in BC. All of our coffee in now roasted in super efficient Loring roasters which use 80% less gas than traditional roasters. Meaning less fossil fuel and less air pollution when compared to our competitors. What does this mean to you? More reasons to love our coffee, and also save a bit of money too!

COMMITMENT TO QUALITY

At Best Gourmet Coffee Company, we’re used to working with all types of customers - local or global, large or small. Exceptional quality is the foundation of our company, and is the driving force behind all decisions; whether it’s which coffee to purchase or the best way to get it fresh to you

All of our coffee is first cupped and approved by our qualified Q grader. This makes sure that coffees with defects don't reach your cup. Next we custom roast and quickly pack our products to insure freshness is sealed in to every bag and can.

Coffee is a highly competitive field, so we’re constantly investing in technology and research to make sure we stay ahead of the trend. Our commitment to quality guarantees our success and your satisfaction.
